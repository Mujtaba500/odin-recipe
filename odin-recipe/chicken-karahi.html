Chicken Karahi

Description

Chicken karahi is a spicy Pakistani and North Indian dish that is made with bone-in chicken pieces cooked in a wok-style pan with tomatoes, ginger, garlic, and a blend of aromatic spices. The dish gets its name from the karahi, a traditional cooking vessel used to prepare the dish. The dish is often garnished with fresh coriander leaves and served with naan or rice.My favourite

Ingredients

  • 1 kg bone-in chicken, cut into pieces
  • 3-4 medium-sized tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 medium-sized onions, finely chopped 2-3 green chilies, sliced lengthwise
  • 1/2 cup of plain yogurt
  • 1 inch piece of ginger, grated
  • 1 tsp of garam masala powde
  • Salt to taste

STEPS

  1. Heat the oil in a karahi or wok over medium-high heat. Add the cumin and coriander seeds and fry for a few seconds until fragrant.
  2. Add the chopped onions and fry until they turn translucent
  3. Add the minced garlic and grated ginger and fry for a minute until fragrant.
  4. Add the chicken pieces and fry until the chicken is slightly browned.
  5. Add the chopped tomatoes, sliced green chilies, red chili powder, turmeric powder, and garam masala powder. Mix well and cook for a few minutes until the tomatoes are soft and pulpy.
  6. Add the yogurt and salt to taste. Mix well and cook for another 10-15 minutes until the chicken is cooked through and the gravy has thickened.
  7. Garnish with chopped fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with naan or rice.
